Measuring Tips

For the most accurate sizing, measure yourself in your underwear or light clothing. Stand with your feet together and maintain good posture while measuring.

  • Bust/Chest: Measure around the fullest part of your bust/chest.
  • Waist: Measure around your natural waistline, keeping the tape comfortably loose.
  • Hip: Measure around the fullest part of your hips.
